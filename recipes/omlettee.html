Omlettee Recipe

Description

An omelette is a simple yet delicious egg dish made by whisking eggs, cooking them in a pan, and often filling them with ingredients like cheese, vegetables, or meats. Light, fluffy, and versatile, omelettes can be customized to suit any taste, making them a popular choice for breakfast or a quick meal. Whether you prefer a classic French omelette, a cheesy American-style omelette, or a fully loaded veggie omelette, this dish is easy to prepare and packed with protein. Serve it with toast, salad, or hash browns for a satisfying meal! 🍳😋

Ingredients

  • 2-3 eggs (raw)
  • 1/4 cup milk or water
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese (optional)
  • 1/4 cup diced vegetables (bell peppers, onions, tomatoes, mushrooms, etc.)

Steps to Make Omlettee

  1. In a bowl, whisk 2-3 eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper. For extra fluffiness, add a tablespoon of milk or water.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and add butter or oil. Pour in the eggs and let them cook for a few seconds, then gently stir while tilting the pan to spread the eggs evenly.
  3. When the eggs are mostly set, add your favorite fillings (cheese, veggies, ham, etc.) to one side. Carefully fold the omelette in half and cook for another 30 seconds. Slide it onto a plate and enjoy! 🍳😋
